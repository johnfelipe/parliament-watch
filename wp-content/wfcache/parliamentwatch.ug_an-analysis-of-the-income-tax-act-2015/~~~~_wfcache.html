An Analysis of the Income Tax Act, 2015

Uganda has a residence based system of taxation, i.e. tax is charged on residents on all their worldwide income. Non-residents are taxed only on income they have sourced in Uganda. Income tax is charged on business income, employment income and property income. The Act provides for various rates of tax
